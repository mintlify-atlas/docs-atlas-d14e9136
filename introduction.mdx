## How It Works

SentimentAPI uses natural language processing to classify customer comments as **POSITIVE** or **NEGATIVE** with probability scores indicating confidence levels.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Create an account and log in to receive your authentication token
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Comments">
    Send individual comments or upload CSV files for bulk analysis
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Results">
    Receive sentiment predictions with probability scores in real-time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Data">
    Download your analyzed results for reporting and decision-making
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why SentimentAPI?

**40% of customers are lost** due to slow response times. SentimentAPI helps you act on what matters:

- **Speed**: Analyze thousands of comments in seconds, not hours
- **Accuracy**: AI models provide confidence scores for each prediction
- **Scale**: Handle bulk uploads up to 3MB for processing large datasets
- **Integration**: Simple REST API for easy integration into existing workflows

<Note>
  SentimentAPI is built with Next.js and powered by advanced AI models to deliver fast, accurate sentiment classification for businesses of all sizes.
</Note>
